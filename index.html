About Us

Excellence in Government Sector Catering

Our Story

Vasati Services Pvt. Ltd., in collaboration with IRCTC, proudly provides daily catering services to the Income Tax Office in Delhi, ensuring hygienic, nutritious, and timely meals that support government employees with quality food and seamless service every day.

Our Commitment

VASATI SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED, incorporated on 28 September 2020, focuses on training and entrepreneurial skills development for youth across India while maintaining excellence in catering services.

Our Vision

To establish Vasati Services as the premier provider of government-sector catering across India, renowned for its unwavering commitment to hygiene, innovation, and excellence, fostering healthier and more productive work environments.

Our Mission

To deliver hygienic, nutritious, and timely catering solutions to government institutions through trusted partnerships, ensuring quality and satisfaction at every meal.
